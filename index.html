The Specs

All CROVER NFTs are dope, but some are rarer than others. They are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS.

The CROVER

Societies of the 50ies were divided into small tight-knit communities mostly comprised out of families. Over the decades this changed from families to friends in larger crowds to more self-focused egoistic performers.

Now, as we have moved into the modern digital era, people are beginning to create connected tribes on an international level - that get mixed and rebuild in new settings before they stabilise and are lasting. The modern tribes share, care and prosper together and for each other: This we call: croverism!

Sharing comes before possession, preservation before destruction, togetherness before selfishness - croverism, the new kind of humanity.

crover (kroh-ver), noun, plural crovers
a variation of people who live and work crossover and combine their travel, life, work and play together
a wide segment of any age and other demographics with a focus on digitalization, decarbonization, development and doing
economically and financially intelligent people who wish to be set free by being able to live and work anywhere on the planet for a steady monthly rent
skilled people who are active in global labor markets with a high interest in smart things, technology, habitats and the metaverse (and all applied tools and services in it)
a new population with a sharing attitude set free to found the croverse (use of the metaverse in an optimised way, but still enjoying a free and real life offline with multitude benefits created just for them)
